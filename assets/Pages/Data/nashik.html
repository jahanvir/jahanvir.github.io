The average cost of living in Nashik is $350, which is in the top 3% of the least expensive cities in the world, ranked 9050th out of 9294 in our global list, 76th out of 220 in India, and 13th out of 21 in Maharashtra.

The median after-tax salary is $362, which is enough to cover living expenses for 1 months. Ranked 6262nd (TOP 67%) in the list of best places to live in the world and 61st best city to live in India, and 8th most liveable city in Maharashtra. With an estimated population of 1.49M, Nashik is the 29th largest city in India.
